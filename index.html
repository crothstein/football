Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is FlagSketch really free to use?

A: Yes. FlagSketch is a free flag football play designer. You can create custom plays, build formations, and export your playbooks without paying for a subscription or downloading expensive software. However, we do offer paid plans if you need advanced features, such as custom team branding on your prints or specialized wristband layouts.

Q: How do I print plays for my wristbands?

A: FlagSketch is designed specifically for wrist coaches. When you finish designing your plays, simply click "Print" and select the "Wristband" option. Our tool automatically formats your plays into a PDF sized perfectly for standard youth and adult wrist coach inserts.

Q: Can I use this on my iPad or Chromebook?

A: Absolutely. FlagSketch is 100% web-based. It works directly in your browser on any device—including iPads, Android tablets, and Chromebooks. No app store download is required.

Q: Do you have templates for 5v5, 6v6, and 7v7 leagues?

A: Yes. We offer pre-built play templates for all major league formats, including 5v5, 6v6, and 7v7. You can start with a blank field or load a standard formation like "Spread" or "Trips" to save time.

Q: Can I save my playbook and edit it later?

A: Yes. Your playbooks automatically save to your account, so you can access them from anywhere. This allows you to edit your strategies throughout the season as your team evolves.
